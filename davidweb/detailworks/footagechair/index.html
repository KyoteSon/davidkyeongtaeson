#Furniture Design

"Restraint," humans are often constrained by a number of factors. I wanted to make a work through this bondage. Inspired by cages and ropes, he designed lighting using two objects. Cages and ropes are representative elements of restraint. Using these two elements, I created a light that I thought was binding on humans from my point of view. I personally believe that humans behavior are controlled under the lights. Specifically, celebrities and broadcasters are controlled for their actions under the light. They can't say what they wants to say or do as their pleases. Also, ordinary people think that they cannot act as they want in a space with light. This work does not escape the bondage, but acknowledges the bondage and expresses it as a work of art.
